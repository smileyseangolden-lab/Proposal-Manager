## Standard Change Management (WOCN) Boilerplate

E Tech Group follows a formal Work Order Change Notice (WOCN) process for managing
scope changes during project execution. All changes are documented, priced, and
approved by the client before work proceeds. This process ensures transparency and
prevents scope creep while maintaining project schedule integrity.

## Standard Risk Library

The following risks are commonly included in MCT proposals. The AI agent should
select applicable risks and supplement with project-specific risks from the RFP.

| Risk | Typical Mitigation |
|------|-------------------|
| Schedule delays due to client-furnished equipment delivery | Early identification of OFCI items; proactive tracking of delivery dates |
| Subcontractor performance | Qualified LV Sub selection; E Tech field supervision; milestone-based payments |
| Scope ambiguity in drawings | RFI process to clarify before execution; documented assumptions |
| Site access constraints | Advance coordination with GC/client; flexible staffing plans |
| Supply chain delays for instruments/panels | Early procurement; maintained supplier relationships; alternate sourcing |
| Design changes during execution | Formal WOCN process; change order documentation |
| Labor availability at project location | Advance recruiting; rotation planning; local labor partnerships |
| Safety incidents | Comprehensive safety program; site-specific training; incident response procedures |
