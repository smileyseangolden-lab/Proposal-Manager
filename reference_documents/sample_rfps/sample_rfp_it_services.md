# REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL

## IT Infrastructure Modernization Services

**Solicitation Number:** RFP-2025-IT-0042
**Issuing Organization:** Metro County Government
**Issue Date:** January 15, 2025
**Due Date:** March 1, 2025, 5:00 PM EST
**Contact:** Jane Smith, Procurement Officer — jsmith@metrocounty.gov

---

## 1. Purpose

Metro County Government is seeking proposals from qualified vendors to modernize
its existing IT infrastructure, including network upgrades, server migration to
hybrid cloud, and implementation of a unified cybersecurity framework.

## 2. Background

Metro County currently operates a legacy on-premises data center supporting 45
departments and approximately 3,200 employees. The existing infrastructure is
approaching end-of-life and does not meet current security and performance
requirements.

## 3. Scope of Work

The selected vendor shall provide the following services:

### 3.1 Network Infrastructure Upgrade
- Replace aging core and distribution switches across 12 facilities.
- Implement SD-WAN to connect all county facilities.
- Upgrade wireless infrastructure to Wi-Fi 6E across all buildings.

### 3.2 Hybrid Cloud Migration
- Assess current workloads and develop a migration plan.
- Migrate eligible workloads to a cloud environment (Azure or AWS).
- Maintain on-premises infrastructure for workloads requiring local hosting.
- Implement backup and disaster recovery in the cloud.

### 3.3 Cybersecurity Framework
- Deploy a Zero Trust architecture.
- Implement Security Information and Event Management (SIEM).
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
- Provide 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring for 12 months.

### 3.4 Training & Knowledge Transfer
- Provide training for county IT staff on new systems.
- Deliver runbooks and documentation for all deployed solutions.

## 6. Proposal Requirements

Proposals shall not exceed 50 pages (excluding resumes and appendices) and must include:

1. Cover letter with authorized signature.
2. Executive summary (2 pages max).
3. Technical approach addressing each scope element.
4. Management plan including project schedule.
5. Staffing plan with resumes of key personnel.
6. Past performance — minimum 3 relevant projects within the last 5 years.
7. Pricing — firm fixed price with itemized breakdown.
8. Compliance matrix mapping each requirement to the proposal section.

## 7. Terms & Conditions

- Contract type: Firm Fixed Price
- Contract term: 12 months with two optional 12-month extensions for SOC monitoring.
- Insurance requirements: $2M general liability, $1M professional liability.
- The County reserves the right to reject any or all proposals.
