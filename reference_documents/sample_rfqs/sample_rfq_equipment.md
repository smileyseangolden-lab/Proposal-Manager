## 1. Purpose

Riverside Unified School District is requesting quotations for the supply and
delivery of office furniture and workstation equipment for the new District
Administration Building.

## 3. Requirements

- All furniture must meet BIFMA standards.
- GREENGUARD Gold certification preferred.
- Minimum 10-year warranty on desks, 5-year on seating.
- Delivery to: 4500 Oak Boulevard, Riverside, CA 92501.
- Delivery must include placement in designated rooms (floor plan provided upon award).
- Assembly required by vendor; no flat-pack delivery.
- Delivery window: April 14 – April 25, 2025.

## 4. Quotation Requirements

Please provide:
1. Unit price and extended price for each item.
2. Manufacturer and model number for each item.
3. Delivery and installation cost (itemized separately).
4. Product specification sheets or catalog pages.
5. Warranty details for each product category.
6. Lead time from order placement.
7. Any available volume discounts.

## 5. Evaluation

Quotations will be evaluated on:
- Total cost (60%)
- Product quality and specifications compliance (25%)
- Delivery timeline and reliability (15%)

## 6. Terms

- Payment: Net 30
- The District reserves the right to award by individual line item or as a total package.
- Tax-exempt — a tax-exempt certificate will be provided upon award.
